# Concept Writing

Lead with the purpose and mental model. Explain what the concept is, why it exists in this system, its boundaries, lifecycle, invariants, and interactions. Separate observed behavior from inferred interpretation. Include implementation references only where they help orientation; do not narrate files one by one.

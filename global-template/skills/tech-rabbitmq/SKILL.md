# RabbitMQ Discovery Heuristics

Identify exchange type, routing keys, queues, bindings, durability, publisher confirms, consumer acknowledgement, prefetch, retry, dead-letter configuration, and RabbitMQ Streams APIs where present. Do not infer delivery guarantees from broker defaults alone.
